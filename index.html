About

Who Is Young Lord Eisen?

Who's the Haus? SKS? The Tales??

"The best things come when you master what you need to, not what you want to.."

For the last 7 years, Eisen has been perfecting his craft with SKS to create and cultivate his own style of music. The self proclaimed and patented genre, "Pyramid Music" consists of very lush harmonies dynamically flowed within various modern and classical styles to bring what Eisen refers to as the "pinnacle of perfected crafts pressed together" .

  • Working With SKS

    Together, two evolved halves of a uncanny whole developed and have curated the first "Tales of Eisen", a collection of projects meant to portray the story of the star soldier YLE and Papo, the mystery man from another world!

  • Working With SKYBOX

    A constantly evolving relationship with a family of creative geniuses has cultivated an environment of exponential growth in which there are no limits to what can be created (and perfected).
